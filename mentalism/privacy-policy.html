Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Mentalism Mastery is designed with your privacy as a priority. Your learning content stays on your device, and analytics are optional and consent-based.

Data Storage

  • All your lesson progress, notes, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No user account or registration is required.
  • We never sell or share your personal data.

Analytics (Optional, Consent-Based)

With your explicit consent, we use Firebase Analytics by Google to understand which features are useful and to fix issues faster. Collection is fully gated on your choice:

  • You are asked to accept or decline analytics on first launch.
  • If you decline, no analytics events are sent — the analytics module is disabled at runtime.
  • You can change your preference at any time from the app's settings.
  • Events are anonymous (e.g. lesson_completed, feature_opened) and never include personal information you enter into the app.
Firebase Analytics is governed by Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Permissions

  • Notifications — Used only for practice reminders you choose to enable.
  • Vibration — Used for haptic feedback on interactions.
  • Internet — Used to load content updates and (if you opt in) send anonymous analytics events.

Data Deletion

You can reset all locally stored data at any time from the app's settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data. To request deletion of any analytics data tied to your device, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Mentalism Mastery is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at segmentbi@gmail.com.
