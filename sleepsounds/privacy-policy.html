Sleep Sounds — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Introduction

Sleep Sounds ("we", "our", "the app") is a relaxing ambient sound mixer developed and published by OVC Tech. We take your privacy seriously. Sleep Sounds is designed to help you relax and sleep better using soothing sounds. This policy explains what data the app uses, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Sound preferences — your selected sounds, volume levels, and timer settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Advertising ID (Google AdMob) — we display ads through Google AdMob, which may collect your advertising ID and device information to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT require account creation or login
  • We do NOT sell or share your personal data

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to serve ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Firebase Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics to help us improve the app. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your sound preferences and mixer settings
  • Timer configurations
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Permissions

  • Internet — Required for loading advertisements.
  • Notifications — Optional. Used for timer notifications.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers.

How to delete your data:

  • Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Sleep Sounds → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
