Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

SpeakUp helps you practice speaking with confidence. Because the app analyzes your voice, this policy explains exactly how your audio is handled.

Audio Recording & AI Analysis

When you start a practice session, SpeakUp records your speech using your device's microphone. To give you coaching feedback (transcript, speaking pace, filler-word count, and an overall score), the recorded clip is sent to Google Gemini for analysis.

  • Recordings are only captured during an active practice session that you start.
  • We do not store your audio on our servers. After Gemini returns the analysis, the clip is no longer needed by us.
  • Processing by Google is governed by Google's privacy policy and Generative AI terms — see policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Only the analysis results (score, transcript text, pace, etc.) are saved to your device for your session history.
Please avoid speaking sensitive personal information (passwords, financial details, health information) during practice sessions, since the audio will be transmitted to Google's AI service for analysis.

Data Storage

  • Your practice history, scores, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No user account or registration is required.
  • We never sell or share your personal data.

Permissions

  • Microphone — Required to record your practice sessions for AI coaching feedback. The app only records while a session is active.
  • Modify Audio Settings — Used to configure the recording stream for clear capture.
  • Vibration — Used for haptic feedback during sessions.

Data Deletion

You can reset your entire practice history at any time from the app's settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data. If you wish to request deletion of any data processed by Google's Gemini service, contact us at the email below and we will help facilitate the request.

Children's Privacy

SpeakUp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
