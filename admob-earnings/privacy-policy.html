AdMob Earnings — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Introduction

AdMob Earnings ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application developed and published by OVC Tech that helps publishers view their Google AdMob earnings and revenue analytics. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Google Account (OAuth Sign-In) — We use Google Sign-In to authenticate you and access your AdMob reporting data via the Google AdMob API. We request read-only access to your AdMob reports. Your Google credentials are never stored by our app — authentication is handled entirely by Google's secure OAuth 2.0 flow.
  • AdMob Earnings Data — We retrieve your AdMob earnings, impressions, clicks, and revenue data from Google's AdMob Reporting API. This data is displayed within the app and cached locally on your device for offline viewing.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT store your Google password or authentication tokens on external servers
  • We do NOT share your AdMob earnings data with any third parties
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT sell your personal data
  • We do NOT access any AdMob data beyond what is needed to display your earnings reports

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cached earnings data for offline viewing
  • Your display preferences and settings
  • OAuth refresh token (encrypted, stored securely on-device)

This data never leaves your device except to communicate with Google's APIs. You can clear it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by signing out.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob API — We use Google's AdMob Reporting API to retrieve your earnings data. This communication is secured via HTTPS and authenticated using OAuth 2.0. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In — Authentication is handled by Google's OAuth 2.0 service. We only receive a token to access your AdMob data — we never see or store your Google password.
  • Google AdMob (Ads) — We display ads served by Google AdMob to support the free version of the app. When an ad is requested, Google and its partners may collect and process:
    • Your Advertising ID (a resettable identifier that you can reset or disable in your device settings)
    • Approximate location derived from your IP address
    • Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
    • Ad-interaction events (impressions, clicks)
    For details, see How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Permissions

  • Internet — Required to communicate with Google's AdMob API and to display ads.
  • Google Account Access — Required to authenticate and fetch your AdMob earnings data.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers.

How long we retain data: Local data (cached earnings, preferences) is kept on your device for as long as the app is installed.

How to delete your data:

  • Sign out from within the app to clear your authentication token and cached data.
  • Go to your device's Settings → Apps → AdMob Earnings → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data from your device.
  • You can also revoke the app's access to your Google Account at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed for or directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
