Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Pulse ("we", "our", or "the App") is a mobile application developed by OVC Tech. Pulse is a personal dashboard that shows the stability (crash-free and ANR-free rates) and install counts of the apps in your own Google Play Console account. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Key Point: Pulse has no backend servers of its own and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking. The Play Console data it retrieves is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to or stored on our servers, nor shared with anyone.

1. Information We Access

Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, the App accesses the following from your Google account:

  • Your name, email address, and profile picture (for display in the App only)
  • An authentication token used to access the Google Play APIs on your behalf

Google Play Console Data (read-only)

The App requests read-only access to your own Play Console data, specifically:

  • Android vitals via the Google Play Developer Reporting API (playdeveloperreporting) — crash rate, ANR (App Not Responding) rate, and related app-quality metrics.
  • Install statistics via Google Cloud Storage, read-only (devstorage.read_only) — the install-report files that Google Play exports to your account's Cloud Storage bucket.

This data is shown to you within the App and stays on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or to any third party.

Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your Google authentication token (in your device's secure storage)
  • App preferences, such as which apps you track and your display settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Fetch the vitals and install metrics for the apps in your own Play Console account
  • Calculate and display a stability score and rankings within the App
  • Keep you signed in between sessions so you don't have to authenticate every time

3. Data Storage and Security

All data processing happens locally on your device. Specifically:

  • No backend servers: We do not operate any servers that receive, store, or process your Play Console data or account information.
  • Secure token storage: Your Google authentication token is stored using your device's secure storage (Expo SecureStore), which uses the Android Keystore system.
  • Local preferences: App settings are stored on-device.
  • No analytics or ads: The App does not use analytics, crash-reporting, or advertising SDKs, and no advertising identifier (AD ID) is collected.

4. Third-Party Services

The App communicates directly with the following Google services from your device:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Developer Reporting API: For retrieving your apps' vitals. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Storage (read-only): For retrieving your install-statistics reports. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with anyone. Your data stays on your device and is transmitted only to Google's APIs to fulfil your own requests, as described above.

6. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained only for as long as you have the App installed.
  • Signing out removes your authentication token and any cached data.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a tool for app developers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App.
  • Delete: Sign out to remove cached data, or uninstall the App to remove all data.
  • Revoke access: You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account permissions.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
