📉 Trace — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Introduction

Trace ("we", "our", "the app") is a private, on-device weight-loss journey tracker developed and published by OVC Tech. We take your privacy seriously. Trace is built to be local-only: the health information you log stays on your device. This policy explains what data the app uses, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Health & fitness data you enter — the weight, body photos, meals, activity, and sleep you log are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Health Connect (optional) — if you grant permission, Trace reads your step count and last night's sleep from Android Health Connect so you don't have to type them in. This data is read on-device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Camera / Photos (optional) — if you add a body photo, Trace accesses your camera or photo library to import that image. Photos are saved in the app's private storage and never leave your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ We do NOT upload your weight, photos, meals, or other health data to any server
  • ❌ We do NOT track your location
  • ❌ We do NOT sell or share your data
  • ❌ We do NOT serve ads of any kind
  • ❌ We do NOT use third-party analytics or trackers
  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or login

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device, using an on-device database (SQLite) and your device's private file storage:

  • Weight entries (in your chosen units, kg or lb)
  • Body photos you add
  • Meals and portion tags
  • Activity (walks, gym, steps) and sleep entries
  • Your goal weight and app settings (units, reminders, app lock)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time from within the app or by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Health Connect (Android)

Trace can optionally connect to Android Health Connect to import your steps and sleep. Access is read-only, happens only after you grant permission, and the data is used solely to fill in your daily log on your device. You can revoke this permission at any time in Health Connect or your device settings. Trace does not write data to Health Connect and does not transmit Health Connect data anywhere.

Permissions

  • Health Connect (steps, sleep) — Optional. Used to import your steps and last night's sleep. You can deny it and still log manually.
  • Camera / Photos — Optional. Used only when you add a body photo.
  • Notifications — Optional. Used for daily logging reminders.
  • Biometric / device unlock — Optional. Used only to unlock the app if you enable the app lock. Authentication is handled by your device; Trace never sees your fingerprint or face data.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to retain or delete.

How long we retain data: local data is kept on your device for as long as the app is installed. There is no expiration — data persists until you choose to delete it.

How to delete your data:

  • Delete individual entries within the app, or
  • Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Trace → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all associated data, including photos, from your device.

A note on backups: Trace lets you export an optional, encrypted backup file that you control. Where that file is stored, and who you share it with, is up to you — we never receive it. If you have any concerns, contact us at segmentbi@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is suitable for general audiences.
