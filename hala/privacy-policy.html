Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Hala is built with a privacy-first approach. All your learning data stays on your device.

Data Storage

  • All lesson progress, saved phrases, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No user account or registration is required.
We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Period.

Permissions

  • Notifications — Used only for daily practice reminders you choose to enable.
  • Vibration — Used for haptic feedback on correct answers and milestones.

Data Deletion

You can reset all your learning data at any time from the app's settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Hala is suitable for general audiences but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at segmentbi@gmail.com.
