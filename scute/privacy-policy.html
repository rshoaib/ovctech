🦎 Scute — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Introduction

Scute ("we", "our", "the app") is a private, on-device reptile husbandry tracker developed and published by OVC Tech. We take your privacy seriously. Scute is built to be local-only: the records you log stay on your device. This policy explains what data the app uses, why, and how it's handled.

Data We Access

  • Care records you enter — the animals, feedings, weights, sheds, husbandry readings (temperature/humidity), and notes you log are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Camera / Photos (optional) — if you add a photo of an animal, Scute accesses your camera or photo library to import that image. Photos are saved in the app's private storage and never leave your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ We do NOT upload your animals, photos, logs, or other data to any server
  • ❌ We do NOT track your location
  • ❌ We do NOT sell or share your data
  • ❌ We do NOT serve ads of any kind
  • ❌ We do NOT use third-party analytics or trackers
  • ❌ We do NOT require account creation or login

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device, using an on-device database (SQLite) and your device's private file storage:

  • Animal profiles (name, species, morph, sex, feeding interval, notes)
  • Feeding, weight, shed, and husbandry log entries
  • Photos you add for an animal
  • App settings (feeding-reminder preferences)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time from within the app or by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Permissions

  • Camera / Photos — Optional. Used only when you add a photo of an animal.
  • Notifications — Optional. Used for per-animal feeding reminders.

Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not store any of your data on external servers, so there is no server-side data to retain or delete.

How long we retain data: local data is kept on your device for as long as the app is installed. There is no expiration — data persists until you choose to delete it.

How to delete your data:

  • Delete individual animals or log entries within the app, or
  • Go to your device's Settings → Apps → Scute → Storage → Clear Data to erase all locally stored app data.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all associated data, including photos, from your device.

A note on backups: Scute lets you export an optional backup file that you control. Where that file is stored, and who you share it with, is up to you — we never receive it. If you have any concerns, contact us at segmentbi@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for a general adult audience.

Changes to This Policy
